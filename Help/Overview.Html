Overview



Whats with the name?
First of all, RWanIP stands for Remote Wide Area Network Internet Protocol (IP) Address.
We also simplify the pronunciation of RWanIP and just pronounce is "Ran I.P.".

What is RWanIP for?
RWanIP is a windows service that will poll your actual external WAN IP address and make it easily available to you in the windows system tray. This service can also upload the obtained IP information to an FTP server so that it can be retrieved from outside your home or office.

This has proven especially useful for companies that have many customers who they support remotely, as they can install RWanIP and consolidate all of their customers IP addresses into one location on their FTP server.
